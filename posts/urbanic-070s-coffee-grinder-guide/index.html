Urbanic 070s Coffee Grinder Guide

Feb 17, 2023

This is a rough guide to which setting to use on the Urbanic 070s grinder for a few different brewing methods, and some recipes I like using with them.

I’ve modded my grinder to be stepless, and set the dial such that it just chirps when the pointer goes below (clockwise from) zero. This is covered in this video from Lance Hedrick.

Recipes are in format: {Recipe} - {creator’s grind description} - {good starting setting}



Aeropress - 8-10

  • Sprometheus’ All-Round Brew Recipe - Medium-fine - 9
  • Tuomas Merikanto’s WAC2021 Winning Recipe - Medium-coarse - 10

V60 - 7-9

I use James Hoffmann’s recipes, which are particular good if you don’t have a gooseneck kettle.

  • 2+ Cup Recipe - Medium-fine - 8.5
  • 1 Cup Recipe - Medium-fine - 8

Cafetière/French Press - 14

  • Hoffmann’s Ultimate French Press Technique - Medium-coarse - 14

Espresso - 2-3

  • Origin’s Guide - Fine - 2
