    Everything I watched in 2025

    2026-03-01

    At the start of 2025, I set myself a goal of watching 24 films over the year. I ended up watching 47! You can see each of the individual titles on my films page.

    Some things that I think helped me exceed my goal:

    • Flying to/from New York in January gave me lots of time to watch some classics (Citizen Kane, finally!) and helped build up some momentum
    • Defaulting to watching films over TV series/Youtube, and being happy to indulge in "easier" films (Total Recall)
    • Having an aversion to hitting my reading goal (24 books, read 12) and watching films to make me feel better

    Having not really made much of a structured plan/film list, I thought it might be interesting to do some very light data analysis on the films I ended up choosing.

    Films watched per month

    January and October were the busiest months. July was the quietest with just one film (Kingdom Of Heaven, slightly disappointing - probably should have watched the Director's Cut).

    By release decade

    The oldest films I watched were:

    1. Citizen Kane (1941)
    2. To Catch a Thief (1955)
    3. Persona (1966)

    TV series

    I also watched 6 series across the year, all of them fantastic:

    • The Wire S1
    • Twin Peaks S1 (twice)
    • Severance S2
    • Veep S1
    • The Last of Us S2
    • The Studio S1
